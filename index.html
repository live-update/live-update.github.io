Our Organization

Established in 1992 in the heart of Nagaon, Majuli, our organization was founded with a unified vision: to foster brotherhood, community spirit, and physical excellence through sports and cultural events. We continuously work towards the development of local youth through major sporting tournaments and cultural gatherings.
